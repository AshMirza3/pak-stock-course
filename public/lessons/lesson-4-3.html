How to Use Technical Analysis on PSX Stocks

Let’s walk through a quick example using a popular Pakistani bank—MCB Bank (Ticker: MCB):

  1. Open your online charting platform (e.g., JS Global’s TraderPlus, or a free site like TradingView).
  2. Load MCB’s daily candlestick chart for the last 6 months.
  3. Overlay the 50-day and 200-day SMA:
    • If the 50-day SMA crosses above the 200-day SMA (“Golden Cross”), that’s often a bullish signal.
    • If the 50-day SMA crosses below the 200-day SMA (“Death Cross”), that’s bearish.
  4. Check RSI on a 14-day basis:
    • If RSI is above 70, MCB may be overbought—proceed with caution on new longs.
    • If RSI is below 30, MCB could be oversold—watch for reversal signs.
  5. Draw support/resistance lines by connecting swing lows (support) and swing highs (resistance). If price repeatedly bounces near PKR 150, that’s a strong support level.

Case Study: In February 2025, MCB’s price formed a double bottom at PKR 147, RSI dipped to 28, then rebounded above 30. Shortly after, price rallied toward PKR 160, validating the oversold bounce.
