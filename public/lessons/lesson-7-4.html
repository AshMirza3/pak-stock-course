Taxation & Regulatory Filings

Understanding taxes on trading is crucial: Capital Gains Tax (CGT):

  • Resident Individual (Unlisted Shares): Exempt if held > 12 months; otherwise 15% on gains if sold within 12 months. (Not applicable to PSX shares—only unlisted companies.)
  • Resident Individual (PSX Shares):
    • Until Junê30, 2023: Exempt for all holding periods.
    • From July 1, 2023 onward, SRO rules may reintroduce CGT on PSX trades depending on holding period (>1 year exemption). Check SECP notifications for updates.
  • Non‐Resident Investor: 15% on profit from PSX trades, withheld at source by CDC.

Withholding Tax & Stamp Duty:

  • Withholding (WHT) on Dividend Income: 15% for resident individuals (unless exemption certificate); 30% for foreign individuals.
  • Stamp Duty: 0.02% of trade value or PKR 10 minimum per side (broker-dealer enters and pays; automatically deducted).
  • SECP Fee: 0.001% of trade value (collected by broker).
  • Exchange Fee: 0.0115% of trade value.
  • CDC Fee: 0.0002% on equity cross transactions.

Regulatory Filings:

  • Annual Tax Returns: Report capital gains/losses in your annual Income Tax Return (ITR) to FBR.
  • Form C for Foreign Investors: If you’re a foreign investor, provide Form C to claim reduced WHT under Double Taxation Agreements.
  • CDSR Annual Statement: CDC issues an annual summary—list of dividend income, CGT, and WHT paid—helpful for ITR filing.

Tip: Always keep contract notes and CDC statements. Consult a tax advisor, as rules can change frequently.
