PSX Structure & Trading Hours

The Pakistan Stock Exchange operates on a standardized schedule, with two main sessions:

  • Pre-Opening Session (9:15 AM – 9:30 AM): Orders are entered but not matched. This allows participants to revise orders before the opening auction.
  • Opening Time (9:30 AM): Orders from the pre‐open book are matched at the equilibrium price, determining the first trade price.
  • Continuous Trading (9:30 AM – 3:30 PM): Market operates with live order matching. All market, limit, and special orders execute based on price/time priority.

Settlement follows a T+2 cycle—meaning if you buy shares on Tuesday, the trade officially settles (funds and shares exchange) on Thursday.

Key Entities:

  • PSX Ltd.: Operator of the exchange infrastructure and trading system.
  • Central Depository Company (CDC): Maintains investors’ share accounts electronically, facilitating settlements and corporate actions.
  • Clearing Company of Pakistan (CCP): Acts as central counterparty, guaranteeing trade settlement.
